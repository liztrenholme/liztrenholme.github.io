About

Hi, I'm Liz! I'm a Front-End Developer working to expand my skillset to Full-Stack. I used to dabble in HTML and CSS as a teenager, but I have since rediscovered and fallen in love with coding in August of 2017. I have been attending the Coding Bootcamp at Case Western Reserve University since January of 2018. As an artist, I love to incorporate creative design into my projects. I am well-versed in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and jQuery, and have most recently been working a lot with React. I am also quite familiar with backend technologies including Node and Express, and database technologies like MySQL and MongoDB. The future will see me expanding on all of this and learning Vue, React Native, and exploring mobile languages like Swift.



Skills
