About

As an artist, I love to incorporate creative design into my projects. I am well-versed in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and jQuery, and have most recently been working with React. Every front-end project I undertake focuses on a "mobile first" design, as modern trends demand a website be accessible and easy to navigate on any device, and taking into account ease of use and reliability for the user is imperative. At the same time though, a solidly constructed back-end that is fast and communicates well with the front-end is just as important.

I'm currently working to expand on my Full-Stack skills. I have been studying JavaScript since August of 2017, and have completed the six month long Coding Bootcamp program at Case Western Reserve University as of July 2018. Backend technologies I have worked with so far include Node and Express, as well as database technologies like MySQL and MongoDB. The future will see me expanding on all of this and exploring Vue, React Native, and mobile languages like Swift.
