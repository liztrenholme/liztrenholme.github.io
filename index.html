About

Hi, I'm Liz! I'm a Front-End Developer. I used to dabble in code as a teenager, but I have since been learning how to code on my own after rediscovering and falling in love with it in August of 2017. I have been attending the Coding Bootcamp at Case Western Reserve University since January of 2018. I'm also an artist and love to incorporate creative design into my projects. I am proficient in HTML, CSS and JavaScript, and familiar with jQuery. In the coming months, I will be devoting my time to learning React, Angular and backend technologies including Node, MySQL and MongoDB.
